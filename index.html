About the Trust

Bilqees Razia Laghari Welfare Trust was established in 2005 with a noble vision to serve the underprivileged communities of Pakistan. Over the past 20 years, the Trust has grown into one of the most active and respected welfare organizations in the country, touching millions of lives through its diverse humanitarian programs.

The Trust was founded by dedicated community leaders who believed that every human being deserves access to healthcare, education, clean water, and a dignified life. Starting from humble beginnings, the Trust has expanded its operations across multiple districts, impacting the lives of the most vulnerable segments of society including widows, orphans, persons with disabilities, and low-income families.

Founding & Leadership History

Our 20-Year Journey of Dedication and Service

2005

Trust Founded

Trust officially founded by Bilqees Razia Laghari with a small medical camp to serve rural communities.

2007

Women Empowerment

First vocational training center established to teach stitching, embroidery, and handicrafts to women.

2009

Scholarship Program Launch

Initiated scholarship program for deserving and underprivileged students from primary to university level.

2011

Health Camps Expansion

Expansion of health outreach programs, initiating specialized Hepatitis screening & Eye cataract surgeries.

2013

Clean Water Projects

Clean Water Project started in rural areas, installing hand pumps and water filtration systems.

2015

Mass Wedding Ceremonies

Launched Combined Marriage Ceremony program to arrange dowry items, food, and wedding setups for poor couples.

2017

HIV Testing and Awareness

Started free confidential HIV testing and educational awareness camps across districts to combat stigma.

2019

Relief & Langar Operations

Blood Rescue & Emergency Relief Operations formally organized to support disaster-struck families.

2020

COVID-19 Relief Package

Emergency Ration Packages distributed to 5,000+ vulnerable families during pandemic lockdowns.

2022

Digital Literacy Program

Computer Education Centers launched to train youth in MS Office, graphic design, and web coding basics.

2024-25

300+ Hepatitis Camps

Successfully completed 300+ Hepatitis camps and reached over 7,000 scholarship beneficiaries across Pakistan.

Our Programs & Community Services

Comprehensive solutions targeting key developmental and basic needs.

Healthcare

General Medical Camps

Free general medical check-up camps organized regularly in rural and peri-urban areas offering OPD consultations, diagnostics, and free medicines.

Healthcare

Eye Camps & Operations

Providing free eye check-ups, glasses, and cataract surgeries. Over 2,800 successful operations have been performed to date.

Healthcare

Hepatitis & HIV Camps

Screening, testing, vaccination, and treatment counseling across regions to manage and prevent Hepatitis and HIV spread.

Education

Scholarship Program

Supporting over 7,000+ students from primary to university level with monthly stipends, fee coverage, books, and uniforms.

Relief & Welfare

Relief & Ration Distribution

During emergencies and times of crisis, the Trust mobilizes Food provision and complete monthly packages to support families.

  • 6,000+ persons fed daily during relief operations.
  • 5,000+ families received complete ration packs (flour, rice, oil, sugar).
Relief & Welfare

Clean Water Project

Installing hand pumps, tube wells, and water filtration plants to deliver safe drinking water to remote water-scarce villages.

  • Installation of hand pumps & tube wells.
  • Construction of water filtration plants.
  • Distribution of water purification tablets and filters.
